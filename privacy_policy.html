Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Overview

FamilyGrove ("we", "our", or "us") is a family organization app built for personal and family use. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the FamilyGrove mobile application.

We take your privacy seriously. FamilyGrove is designed to keep your family's data private and secure — we do not sell your data, display ads, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and display name (used for authentication)
  • Profile photo (optional, uploaded by you)
  • Google account information if you sign in with Google

Family Data You Create

  • Family member profiles (names, dates of birth, physical descriptions)
  • Medical records (allergies, conditions, medications, immunizations)
  • Gift and budget information
  • Household inventory and maintenance records
  • Tasks, calendar events, and shopping lists
  • Photos uploaded to profiles

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system (for app functionality)
  • Crash reports and performance data (via Firebase)

Google Calendar (Optional)

If you enable Google Calendar sync, we access your Google Calendar events in read-only mode to display them in the app. We do not store, modify, or share your Google Calendar data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the FamilyGrove app
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To store and sync your family data across devices
  • To send push notifications for tasks and events (if enabled)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of the services required to operate the app.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Firebase is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure.

  • Firestore — stores your family data
  • Firebase Storage — stores uploaded photos
  • Firebase Auth — manages authentication

Only you can access your data. We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure your data is only readable and writable by your authenticated account.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Google Firebase — our backend infrastructure provider
  • Google Sign-In — if you choose to authenticate with Google
  • Law enforcement — only if required by law or to protect safety

Your Rights & Choices

  • Access — you can view all your data within the app
  • Delete — you can delete individual records or your entire account
  • Export — contact us to request a copy of your data
  • Notifications — you can disable push notifications in Settings
  • Google Calendar — you can disconnect Google Calendar at any time in Settings
  • Biometric — you can disable biometric lock at any time in Settings

Children's Privacy

FamilyGrove is designed for adults to manage family information. While the app stores information about children (as family members), it is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
